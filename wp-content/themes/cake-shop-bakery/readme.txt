== Description ==

Cake Shop Bakery is a free WordPress theme that comes with an intuitive and fascinating design. You can create amazing websites for your bakery, ice cream, pastry, bread shop, cupcake store, pastry house, cake studio, cookies, and baked delicacies, and donuts and muffins shop with this theme. The layout is highly customizable and brings you any personalization options making it easy for you to tweak. With minimal design focussing on the key elements and info displayed on your website, you will also like the user-friendly interface that this retina-ready theme brings highlighting each and every baked product that you display on your site. The theme is capable of adjusting its layout depending upon the screen size of any given divide to give a phenomenal experience to the users and this possible thanks to its responsive design. The sophisticated and clean codes work well for the SEO as well as performance ground and make sure that you also get a secure environment for your website. This beautiful theme is technically advanced and has the Bootstrap framework as its base. Call to Action Button (CTA) and social media options are also a part of this theme to make provisions for conversion and promotions. It is made translation-ready and optimized codes result in faster page load time and also there are various sections such as testimonials, team, and blog with stunning CSS animations.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Cake Shop Bakery in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
